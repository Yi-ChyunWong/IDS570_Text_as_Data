#Topic	Social Media
#Answer
Social Media can affect individuals and the society in different ways. Addiction is a huge problem along many teenagers and young adults as they spend most of their time using different applications to engage and communicate with friends or even new people all around the world. Applications many of us use on a daily  basis like Snapchat, Instagram and twitter are a very easy and effective way to quickly communicate with others however every individuals time should be managed and you can easily do that in the settings of any iphone by controlling how many hours you'd want to spend on social media. That makes a positive impact on our society as well as individuals as they wouldn't be as attached to their devices and would make some time for other activities and hobbies in their daily routines. By doing this there is a bigger chance that young adults would focus more on their studies and work making them achieve a successful future for themselves. In contrast, There are many positive points on social Media as it can be used for studying and teaching purposes or even as a place for advertising many small bussiness of various Emirati's who would like to expand their businesses. Social Media can benefit us only if we use it properly and manage our time with it so that it does not distract us from building a great future for ourselves.
