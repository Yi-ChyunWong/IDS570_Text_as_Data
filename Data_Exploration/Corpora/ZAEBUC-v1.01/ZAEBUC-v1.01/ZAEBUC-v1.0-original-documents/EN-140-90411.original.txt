#Answer
   Social media plays an important role in our society. Social media has changed our lives in many different ways. Most people use social media as a way of communicating with others. Its effects not only individuals as well as the society itself. Nowadays technology has enhanced the way of communication . People don't tend to talk face-to-face anymore.In addition, individuals started using social media as a way of showing their happiness or sadnesess. Social media can affect the person both physically and mentally. For instance, social media can be a way to track sports news and may lead the person to be physically helathy. However, social media can also affect a person mentally. It can lead to depression , anexiety or even sucidal thoughts. The society itself can be destructed by social media. It can spread hateness among people in the society. Social media does affect the way a society would work and represent itself between other communities. It can either bring them closer toward a strong community or pull them down toward a weak community. Most importantly social media have had an affective way of upgrading the community and an individual's mentality towards many objects. The world wouldn't be what it is without social media.
