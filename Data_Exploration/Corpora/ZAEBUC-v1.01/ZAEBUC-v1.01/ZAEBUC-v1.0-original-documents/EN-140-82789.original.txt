#Topic	Social Media
#Answer
In the last decade we were introduced to the newest technology that has completely changed our lives both negatively and positivity. Social media has made life so much easier from many aspects, it made it easier for people to communicate and for people to study from many sources and made entertainment available right in our homes, on the other hand, a lot of people misused social media. Teenagers and children have been provided with smart phones and other types of devices and there is no limit of use to them. Social media consumed the lives of many, problems like losing confidence, lack of sleep, losing temper, loss of memory, and becoming less social as well as being dependent, all of this can be prevented if parents were more strict about how long their children use social media, allowing them only to use it for 2-4 hours instead of 8 hours can make a huge behavioral and social difference in the children's characteristics.  Not only children can be affected by social media, but also adults. Older women and men can face many issues with themselves, especially women, whom tend to wander on social media and browse other women's pages, and start lowering their self-esteem by comparing their own bodies and occupations to other women. There are men all over the word working to support their families financially, but there are men that over-work to provide their family more than enough and forget the importance of bonding, they will keep taking phone calls and emails even when they're back home and never spend crucial time with their loved ones. All in all, social media can be really useful and help you in many ways, but only if you use it the right way, and it can be harmful if you use it the wrong way.
