#Answer
The world we live in is a world of technology, we use it in our day to day life. A part of this world is social media. It became so important that we cannot live without it. But we should recognize the positive and negative affects that social media platforms has on us.  Social media became a huge part of our lives, we stay on our phones most of the day just scrolling down social medias. But do we know the affect it has on us? One of the positive affects on us is that it makes us closer to people. Through social media platforms, we can meet new people from all around the world, we can learn from them about their culture, and we can also teach them about ours. But if we become addicted to these social media platforms, it could destroy our lives. A negative affect about those platforms is the rumors and fake news that spread very quickly through them, and sadly, a lot of people believe those rumors that can destroy the lives of others. That is why we should be careful when using social media, so we don't fall in its deep black hole.  In my opinion, social media platforms are really important in my life. Without them I wouldn't be able to connect with my friends from around the world. But as a user of social medias, I know its negative and positive impacts on me, and my society should know them too so it wouldn't turn into a bad influenced society.
