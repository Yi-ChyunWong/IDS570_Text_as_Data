Not a single day passes were people don,t browse their social media pages. Nowadays everyone knows what the other is doing even when they are miles apart with just a single click.      People have become more shallow over the years as the use of social media increased drasticly. Individuals seem to care more about their apperances so the pictures they would take and upload on social media for others to see, would be well precieded. Their lives have no longer become spontanous and humble. Owning the fanciest cars or bags have become a priority to many individuals. Those that follow such standards are the most followed on social media pages and are recieved with likes on their posts. Society have developed social pressure on individuals to always look a certain way and dress or act in a certain manner. It is liked they are caged in world were they live for others.     Everyone uses social media extensively and most of them don't get much use out of it. At all times whether they are at work, university, home or right before they sleep, people check their phones and open social media. As a society it would be more beneficial for individuals if they would back off social media a bit and not make it the ceneter of their attention.
