a)  Tolerance, one of the most important things that are needed for a society to function properly. Without tolerance, we wouldnt be where we are today the melting pot of cultures that come together and coexist as one. The United Arab Emirates is a country that is known for its tolerance and acceptence, to further emphasise this, the year 2019 has been named the year of tolerance by the ruler of the UAE.  What a ideal way to promote tolerance in the country when the world around us is falling to its feet, when racism has become the new fashion and when the number of hate crimes has risen in the most developed countries in the world.   We as a country cannot stress enough the importance of tolerance in our daily lives, we even have a minister of tolerence that goes to lengths to satisfy the needs of the many religions and nationalities in our country as they too are a crucial pillar in society. The UAE is already doing a great job in promoting tolerance in the country as it is instilled in us, taught in our schools and shown to us on a daily basis. I am very fortunate to live in a country that goes lengths to ensure the happiness of its residence regardless of age, gender, religion or ethnicity.
