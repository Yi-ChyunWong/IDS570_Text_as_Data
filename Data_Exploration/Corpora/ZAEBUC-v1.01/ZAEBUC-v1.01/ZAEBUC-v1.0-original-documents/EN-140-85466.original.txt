#Topic	Social Media
#Answer
No matter where you are, you'll always see people scrolling through their phones either checking up the latest trends or "Snapchatting" their moments to other people. Have you ever wondered if such little applications could cause a big impact on people?   Social media has been one of the most used things in this generation, even the elders of every family have caught up on it. Snapchat, Instagram, Facebook; these applications may seem useless and complex but in reality, people have been spending endless hours on them. Little do they know, it's destroying their lives. The impact Socia media has on individuals is that it could cause loss of connection with others in real life, hostility, and mostly bullying which, unfortunately, increased the rate of suicide of teenagers. Social media gives people a false image on the perfect life, it encourages people to hide behind masks and pretend to be someone they're not. Infact, social media causes the society to follow unethical trends, which could possibly cause violence in the environment. However, there are some benefits that come from Social media, it helps people or family reconnect through video calls or messages that help people build stronger bonds. It also helps people learn new traditions and culture.  In conclusion, Social media has more negative effects than positive ones, which means people should watch out and start using them less frequently to avoid being an internet addict.
