The effect of Social Media on society has increased in the last couple of years. What is social media? Social media consists of applications that help you connect with others, share pictures with them, and even more. Social Media has become a huge part  of our lives, and some people cant go one day without using it. Social Media has effected society in good ways and bad ways.    Many adults, teenagers, and kids have become addicted to life on social media to the point where they can't go hours without using their phones. Some people have also become emotionless while using social media; for example if someone doesn't like the other they immediately block them. The worst affect Social Media has on society is that it distracts people about whats happening around them in the present time. Generations are becoming more dependent on technology and social media that they forget to live their own life.    Although Social Media has great negative effects on society it also has many positive advantages for example; communication has become easier in so many ways with applications like whatsapp, twitter, and skype.
