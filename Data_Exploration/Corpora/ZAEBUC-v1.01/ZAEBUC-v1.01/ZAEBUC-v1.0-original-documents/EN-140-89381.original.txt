Social media has a huge impact on societies in both positive and negative way. First, the positive effects. Social media can be a great way of communication and allow you to contact and stay in touch with long distance friendships you can also be updated with the news and new trends not only news but also a way of learning and getting knowladge as well as following people you love such as authors, influencers and more. Somehow, social media can be  usefull for enterpreneurs and business because you can have advirtisment for free. on the other hand, social media can have a negative influence on people and societies. By spreading rumors and fake news. social media can take you away from your family and friends because we no longer have face to face chats and gatherings instead we just send a text message or a call to ask about our beloved ones. It can also take you away from your hobbies and interests because nowdays people  spend their free time scrolling down time lines and watching posts and videos all over social media applications instead of reading a book, painting or any other hobby. Afterall, we can choose how we can be effected by social media. If we used it in a good way it will have a good and positive influence and if we misused it it will effect us in a negative way.
