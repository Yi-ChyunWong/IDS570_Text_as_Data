However, our community is facing a fact that tolerance can be appllied only for a certain time as we are in the year of tolerance, here in the UAE.   Moreover, in the UAE, tolerance is so important for the society, our ruleres decide that this year "2019" is the year of tolerance. This year in the UAE, we made a lot of talking circles and advicing sessions to let people in the UAE know how much important is it, we started promoting more about this subject in schools, offices and also in the tourist places.   H.H. We should show them how respectful we are, we should work on ourselves and be achievers.   In conclusion, tolerance is a fact that we should follow in our life always either in our community or with others abroad.
