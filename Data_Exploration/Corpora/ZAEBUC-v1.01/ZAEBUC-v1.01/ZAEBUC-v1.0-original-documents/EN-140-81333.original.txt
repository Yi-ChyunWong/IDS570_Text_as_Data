a) In my personal opinion, the UAE already promotes a culture of tolerance by having a spectrum of cultures mixed together. The UAE is one of the most diverse places I know, I grew up in an environment where I learned tolerance and acceptance from a young age. Tolerance and acceptance is one of the side effects of growing up in a diverse culture and society, being born into a world full of different faces, backgrounds, and races. Learning to love all and tolerate all comes easy in a place like the UAE and I've never been more proud to call a place home. Everyone in the world can become more tolerable, there is no place that has achieved maximum tolerance, but it is a significant place where UAE is starting on its path of tolerance.
