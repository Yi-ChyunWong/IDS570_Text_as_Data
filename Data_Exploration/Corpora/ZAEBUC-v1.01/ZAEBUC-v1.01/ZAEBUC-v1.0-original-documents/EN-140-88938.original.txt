How does social media affect individuals and society?  Social media has various affects on individuals and society that are both positive and negative. Although the word "social" is in the title, interactions between people have been occurring digitially, meaning face to face interactions are lost. People have become drawn to their phones, as if they are addicted to them, thus makng them antisocial. Social media is a good place to allow self expression, for example, Instagram is a platform that allows users the freedom to post pictures of their lives and edit them using their desired filters and editing it in their own way. Over time, people have grown creatively as they take inspiration from other instagrammers and use it themselves. Social meda has also provided job opportunities by sponsoring social influencers to promote products to their followers. However, this comes with the negative backlash from some people which creates pressure for the influencer as they feel like an expectation has been set that they did not reach. This also translates to society whereby social media creates standards of women for example. On social platforms women are presented as skinny, tall, and beautiful. Society then takes these conventons of women and pressures girls about what is expected from them and when they can't meet those expectations they are overwhelmed by feelings of insecurity and sadness which leads to mental illnesses and eating disorders. On the other hand, these typical beauty conventions that social meda displays has also led to the formation of a new society that rebels these expectations and teach women that it is okay not to follow the rules of beauty because everyone is beatiful in their own way.
