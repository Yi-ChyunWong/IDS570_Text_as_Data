How Does Social Media Affect Individuals and Society?  Social Media is a big world that's hidden in our devices. This big world has many affects on people that we don't actually realize, there are positive and negative affects on individuals and on the society. Social media can be very harmful that people reach the level of suicide, and thats from the bullying and shaming it brings to people, and some people in social media hide their identity to do illegal activities. Addiction to Social media is a big issue specially nowadays that even children have devices and can access social media.In this side of the topic, there are endless positive and negative outcomes, like pulling people away from their families and friends yet keeping them in touch only by social media. The only connection to their families who live in the same building can only be by social media, and that causes people to be in a hole all by themselves with their devices and not seeing the actual real world. However, there are still a few positive outcomes. Social media can connect you to people from other countries, and lets you meet new people which actually helps people who are anti social, but too much of it can make you anti social if you're not.It can also pave a way to bringing new ideas or give your thoughts and opinions on situations to the public. Businesses even use social media, in fact, it's probably the best way to advertise since your just a touch away from the whole world. I personally use social media a lot, but I try managing or balancing my time with the outside, and i feel like thats the best way nowadays since social media is a huge part in our day to day lives.
