How do social media affect individuals and society?     In this time and age social media has become an important factor of our daily lives. From a six year old kid to 60 year old man most people seem to be addicted to them. Since social media has become what it is today it has had many affects on individuals and society. When it was first introduced it was a mean to communicate with friends and family and to meet new people. Unfortunatly social media has taken a dark turn, nowadays social media has become more of a weapon than a communating platform  It is used and abused by news channels to brainwash people or by politicians to impose their opinion. It also allowed people with no knowledge or experience in any given field to voice their opinion and talk about things they don't know about. Although there are some negative affects to social media there are also some positive ones. For example many people launch ''hashtags'' to support a certain cause and this has proven effective, the most recent example is that of the amazon fire when millions of social media activist urged goverments to help stop the fire and man responded. Overall social media has both negative and positive affects on both idividuals and the society itself.
